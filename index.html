Special

Sentinel

You have mastered techniques to take advantage of every drop in any enemy’s guard, gaining the following benefits:

  • When you hit a creature with an opportunity attack, the creature’s speed becomes 0 for the rest of the turn.
  • Creatures within 5 feet of you provoke opportunity attacks from you even if they take the Disengage action before leaving your reach.
  • When a creature within 5 feet of you makes an attack against a target other than you (and that target doesn’t have this feat), you can use your reaction to make a melee weapon attack against the attacking creature.

Cunning Action

Starting at 2nd level, your quick thinking and agility allow you to move and act quickly. You can take a bonus action on each of your turns in combat. This action can be used only to take the Dash, Disengage, or Hide action.

Natural Explorer (The Mournland)

When you make an Intelligence (Investigation, Nature) or Wisdom (Animal Handling, Perception, Survival) check while in the Mournland, your proficiency bonus is doubled if you are using one of those skill that you're proficient in. While traveling for an hour or more in the Mournland, you gain the following benefits:

  • Difficult terrain doesn't slow your group's travel.
  • Your group can't become lost except by magical means.
  • Even when you are engaged in another activity while traveling (such as foraging, navigating, or tracking), you remain alert to danger.
  • If you are traveling alone, you can move stealthily at a normal pace.
  • When you forage, you find twice as much food as you normally would.
  • While tracking other creatures, you also learn their exact number, their sizes, and how long ago they passed through the area.

Background (Noble)

Keero, of house Cannith, is the oldest of two sons of Cranmine. His family has owned several factories in the area that are responsible for making everything from household goods to even some parts used in the lightning rail. He was destined for a life of relative ease amond the upper middle class of the area and could easily spend the rest of his days attending local socity events and managing the family business.

This vision of the future that his parents so well nurtured bored Keero and he instead wanted adventure. In his swardsmanship and archary training, which he was expected to be proficient in even though no one in his family can remember when they last to fight or hunt, he dreamed of fighting Kobolds and defending a tower from an onslaught of goblins.

When he learned that "Martin's Magnificent Merchants" was in need of recruits he found his chance. He told his friends and parents he was going on an adventure with the group to some ruins. "You'll come to your senses", his parents exclaimed but let him go nonetheless.
